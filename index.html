Welcome to Topotrainer

Test your knowledge of historical geography by clicking locations on the map.

  1. Upload a .txt quiz file — one question per line in the format:
    Where is Rome? | Rome
  2. Upload a .geojson file with the map features to click on.
  3. A question appears — click the correct location on the map.
  4. Harder questions are repeated more often based on your history.
